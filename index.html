About Temple Run

Temple Run is this awesome endless runner game where you play an explorer escaping from crazy demon monkeys. You swipe to jump, slide, and turn. It's super fun and addictive!

Why I Love It

  • Cuz i can beat my sibling in it and that's just amazing
  • It's easy to pick up but hard to master
  • The graphics are cool and the music gets you pumped(Actually I think the music in "temple run 2- normal first version" is the best among them all)
  • Trying to beat my high score is a blast
  • New characters and power-ups keep things fresh

My Top Tips

  1. Focus on the path ahead, not your character
  2. Use power-ups wisely, especially the shields
  3. Practice makes perfect - don't get frustrated!
  4. Try to memorize obstacle patterns
  5. So keep your cool, and enjoy the game
